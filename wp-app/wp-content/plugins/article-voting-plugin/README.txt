This plugin helps collect votes from users for articles.

== Description ==
A vote widget that has two buttons labeled 'Yes' and 'No' respectively is displayed when an article is viewed in the front end. Visitors can cast
their vote once per article clicking either of those buttons.

Article authors can view the visitor votes result when they open their article for edit in the admin area.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `article-voting-plugin.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place `<?php do_action('plugin_name_hook'); ?>` in your templates
